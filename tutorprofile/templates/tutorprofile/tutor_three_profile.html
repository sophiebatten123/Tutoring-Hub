About Me:

English is a beautiful subject with the potential to let students express themselves and their emotions. However, without guidance it can be a very overwhelming subject for many. During my lessons I help students express their feelings through written text, explore and delve into incredible texts and analyse its meaning. My passion for english shines through in every lesson leaving students feeling confident and interested to know more.

I love rugby and am a member of the mens county team. At the weekends I regularly play games and keep fit at the gym. Alongside this I do puzzles and paint to relax and unwind in the evenings.

As your tutor I will scaffold your understanding leaving you feeling more confident answering those tricky questions. Each lesson will be orientated around your current understanding and we will build upon this. Please bring any questions you have to the lesson and we can work through these together.
