About Me:

Hi, I am Barry and I am a Mathematics Tutor here at TutorHub. I have been teaching maths for many years and am currently Head of Mathematics at a Secondary School in Orton. I love teaching and helping students to achieve their dreams. During my lessons I let students ask questions regularly and direct the lesson at their own pace. Now more than ever it is important we listen to them as students and give them the space to flourish.

I am a keen biker and regularly go out on in the countryside at weekends. I also enjoy spending time with my beautiful family and watching my children grow up. I am easy going and try not to take life too seriously.

Understanding of maths does not happen over night, therefore in my lessons I try to scaffold pupils' answers and guide them towards the correct answer. This not only builds confidence, but gives them the time and space to progress in a relaxed environment. I really look forward to meeting you in our lessons!
