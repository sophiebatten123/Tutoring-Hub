About Me:

I have been tutoring and teaching for over 15 years, currently leading as Standards and Progress Leader at my school. Students feel comfortable when working with me and are confident that they can ask any question, no matter how silly they may perceive it to be. I always try to think of relevant and engaging ways to tackle a topic to enable all abilities to understand. I have access to lots of resources that I share during lessons to back up the learning.

Alongside teaching I regularly spend time with my friends and family, participate in swimming competitions and bake cakes! I pride myself on my approachable nature and friendly attitude to everyone I meet. I hope that this makes you feel more confident in lessons and that you enjoy learning with me!

My lessons are usually orientated around pupil misconceptions, so please feel free to bring along any questions you have struggled with in lessons and we will work through these together. If you need help outside of lessons I will try to accomodate for this and guide you if needed.
