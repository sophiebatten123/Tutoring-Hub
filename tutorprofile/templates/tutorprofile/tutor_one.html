About Me:

I am a recent graduate from Manchester University with a degree in Chemistry and am also able to teach Maths and Physics to 'A' level. I have been told that my approach to teaching makes it enjoyable for my students as I am considered patient and very approachable. I am happy to travel up to 15 miles from my home and am currently available evenings and week-ends.

I am a recently retired Head teacher of a Primary School and have more than 35 years experience of teaching of pupils of all ages. Since retiring, I have given private tuition to a number of pupils with excellent results. I always have the highest expectations of pupils and always have had, even when it was fashionable for learning to be more "child centred". This is an approach I have found to pay rich dividends in terms of a pupil's academic and personal development.
